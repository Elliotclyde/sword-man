About This Dungeon Eternal

Game Overview

This Dungeon Eternal is a fast-paced HTML5 hack-and-slash dungeon crawler game. Battle through endless dungeons as a soldier, master combat mechanics, and face increasingly difficult enemies. Built with Phaser.js and playable directly in your browser.

Gameplay Features

  • Fast-paced action combat with real-time combat mechanics
  • Dash mechanic to evade enemies and obstacles
  • Progressive difficulty with multiple challenging levels
  • Responsive controls optimized for both desktop and mobile
  • Health and damage system with strategic gameplay elements
